# Fertility Fights: The Underhive Pits

**A dystopian turn-based combat simulator set in the brutal penal arenas of 2142.**

---

## The World of Fertility Fights

### The Setting

The year is 2142. Beneath the gleaming spires of the hyper-corporate megastate lies the Underhive — a sprawling, lawless netherworld where the only currency that matters is blood, spectacle, and breeding stock.

The citizenry above live in quiet, seething resentment of the futanari underclass: naturally tall, beautiful, muscular, and sexually virile beings who exist entirely outside the subjugated parameters of traditional gender. They are apex predators — elite athletes built for dominance, independence, and lethal grace. Their very existence is a transgression against the social order.

The megastate's answer is the **Penal Arena**.

### The Penal Arena

Deep in the Underhive pits, condemned futanari gladiators are stripped of their freedom and forced into a spectacle designed to systematically destroy everything they are. Before each match, fighters are subjected to a battery of engineered injections:

- **Synthetic Male Gametes (Strain 88.4)** — Artificially inseminated via pre-match catheterization. Genetically engineered for hyper-motility and immediate, aggressive implantation. Formulated to rapidly gestate demanding, macrosomic male fetuses.
- **Trauma-Linked Gestation Accelerant** — A proprietary biochemical agent that binds fetal development directly to adrenaline spikes and localized abdominal trauma. Every blow to the belly mathematically advances the pregnancy by months.
- **Hucow-Variant Prolactin Stimulant** — A brutal glandular override targeting the host's firm pectoral tissue, forcibly initiating extreme lactation and aggressively expanding the mammary glands to painful, sloshing proportions.
- **Metabolic Cannibalization Catalyst** — A hormone trigger that prioritizes fetal safety over host mobility, actively dissolving high-density athletic muscle mass to fuel rapid fetal growth and dumping excess calories into dense, heavy fat deposits across the hips, thighs, and breasts.

### The Tragedy

The fighters do not break. They do not cower or submit. They are elite warriors trapped in a nightmare, and they rage against it to the bitter end.

But their bodies betray them.

As combat rages and blows land, the engineered parasites inside them panic, thrash, and vent potent hormones into the bloodstream. Lean muscle is strip-mined. Hard, unfeminine armor dissolves and is instantly replaced by pillowy, estrogenic fat. Joints swell and buckle. Milk bloats firm pectorals into aching, drooling liabilities. And through it all, an agonizing, involuntary lust builds — threatening to melt the fighter's mind into a spasming, humiliating climax on the blood-soaked canvas.

This is the core of **Fertility Fights**: the unbroken will versus the traitorous flesh. The fighter's mind remains defiant, furious, unbroken — but Willpower is finite, and the body's surrender is inevitable. The only question is how long you can hold out.

## Game Mechanics

### Combat Structure

Fertility Fights is a **turn-based tactical combat simulator** pitting two futanari gladiators against each other in the Penal Arena.

- **5 actions per round**, up to **12 rounds** per match
- **Tactical range management** across three distances: Long Range, Medium Range, and Close Range
- **Knockdown system**: 3 downs triggers a TKO

### The Action System

Each turn, you choose three things:

| Choice | Options |
|---|---|
| **Target** | **Face** (HP damage), **Tits** (arousal/milk spike), **Belly** (stamina drain + fetal agitation) |
| **Movement** | **Retreat** (increase distance), **Hold** (maintain range), **Advance** (close distance) |
| **Action** | Strike, grapple, guard, or brace (varies by range and fitness) |

### Strike Types

| Strike | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Jab** | Long / Medium | Fast, high accuracy |
| **Straight** | Long / Medium | Heavy damage, slower |
| **Hook** | Medium | Powerful arc strike |
| **Uppercut** | Close | Devastating damage (can be blocked by a heavy belly) |
| **Grapple / Clinch** | Close | Forces arousal buildup |
| **Guard / Brace** | Any | Restores stamina and willpower |

### The Six Tracks of Degradation

Your fighter's body is under siege from six biological vectors, each with escalating stages of ruin:

#### Fetal Size (The Parasites)
*Emotional core: Resentment and biological theft.*

The macrosomic male fetuses are not passive passengers — they are greedy, skittish agents of destruction that actively strip-mine the host's strength to build their own bodies. Their crushing weight destroys mobility, ruins the center of gravity, and bears down on the pelvic floor with agonizing pressure.

| Stage | Months | Weight | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minor Encumbrance | 0–3 | ~5–10 lbs | Dense knot in the abdomen. First flutters of disgust. |
| Moderate Encumbrance | 4–6 | ~15–25 lbs | Visible bump. Shifted center of mass. Internal kicks ruin focus. |
| Heavy Encumbrance | 7–9 | ~35–45 lbs | A 40 lb wrecking ball. Constant sloshing fights momentum. Wide-legged stance forced. |
| Crippling Encumbrance | 10–12+ | ~55–65+ lbs | Biomechanically catastrophic. Incontinence on impact. Involuntary arousal from prostate grinding. |

#### Voluptuousness (Breederfat)
*Emotional core: Humiliation and impediment.*

The public, highly visible stripping of power. Hard muscle dissolves into pillowy, dimpled fat — an extreme, obscenely sexual hourglass forced upon the fighter by hormonal cascade. Permanently spikes stamina exertion and ruins defensive ability, though the padding absorbs some blunt force.

| Stage | Value | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Minor Softening | 0–24 | Vascularity fades. Athletic frame mostly intact. |
| Transitional Plumping | 25–49 | Abs soften. Pectorals bloom into full breasts. Punches lose snap. |
| Heavy Adipose Conversion | 50–74 | 30+ lbs of cellulite. 7.5 lb sloshing udders drag the chest. Thighs chafe. |
| Extreme Deposition | 75+ | 10+ lb breasts rest on the belly. Arms trapped by suffocating titmeat. Movement reduced to waddle-slaps. |

#### Strain (Joint Pulverization)
*Emotional core: Anxiety and the double cruelty.*

Pregnancy's "double cruelty" — joints become simultaneously stiff and inflamed from edema (fluid retention) while being chemically melted by relaxin into loose, unstable rubber. Every punch becomes a gamble. High strain destroys guard ability, lowers knockdown threshold, and causes recoil damage from missed strikes.

| Stage | Value | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Baseline Integrity | 0–19 | Joints only ache after extreme exertion. Full confidence. |
| Onset of Doubt | 20–49 | Ankles swell stiff. Knees feel unnervingly loose. |
| Inflammation & Instability | 50–79 | Moving requires active Willpower. Pivoting sends shooting pain through pelvis. |
| Agony of Movement | 80+ | Connective tissue is a puffy, bloated mess. Any punch risks catastrophic joint failure. |

#### Arousal (The Edging Threat)
The pregnancy hormones generate agonizing, mind-melting lust. Tit strikes and grapples drive arousal upward. At **70+ Arousal**, the fighter's brain melts into **SAVAGE HEAT** — involuntary, uncontrollable rutting behavior. An involuntary climax violently agitates the fetuses for massive growth and deals severe damage.

#### Milk Bloat (Slosh Balloons)
Firm pectorals swell into painfully heavy, sloshing balloons that leak warm cream across the canvas. Milk bloat constantly drains health, penalizes all striking momentum, and generates friction-based arousal spikes. Can be managed via industrial pump at pit stops — at the cost of arousal.

#### Agitation (Fetal Stress)
Belly strikes agitate the skittish fetuses, who panic-thrash and vent stress hormones that accelerate the host's physical degradation. Agitated fetuses strip away muscle mass and force the metabolic shift into fat — the fighter is forced into a humiliating pantomime of protective motherhood, shielding a belly full of parasites she despises.

### Willpower (Defiance)

Willpower is your fighter's last line of defense against biological catastrophe. It is a finite resource (max 100) that can be spent to:

- **Override knockdowns** — Lock buckling knees through sheer spite
- **Interrupt involuntary climax** — Bite through the agony and hold back
- **Break out of Savage Heat** — Wrench the mind free from hormonal hijacking

Willpower is restored by using **Guard/Brace** actions. The tragedy of every match is the inevitability of that Will running dry — leaving an unbroken mind trapped inside a body that has completely surrendered.

### Pit Stops

Between each round, you choose **1 of 2 triage actions** from a rotating selection:

| Action | Benefit | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Caloric Slop** | Restores HP and Stamina | Increases Fat and Strain |
| **Relieve Bladder** | Restores Stamina | — |
| **Apply Cockring** | Prevents orgasm for next round | — |
| **Industrial Pump** | Drains Milk Bloat | Increases Arousal |

Every pit stop choice is a devil's bargain — short-term survival purchased at the price of long-term degradation.

### Victory & Defeat

| Condition | Result |
|---|---|
| **3 Knockdowns** | TKO — automatic loss |
| **HP reaches 0** | Knockout |
| **Knock out your opponent** | Victory |
| **Survive all 12 rounds** | Victory by endurance |

The match concludes with a **branching epilogue** — your choices and your fighter's final condition determine the narrative outcome.

## How to Download & Play

### Prerequisites

- **Node.js** 16 or higher (LTS recommended)
- **npm** (included with Node.js)

### Installation

```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/sentarot/FF-Combat-Simulator.git
cd FF-Combat-Simulator

# Install dependencies
npm install
```

### Running the Game

```bash
# Start the development server
npm run dev
```

This launches a local server. Open `http://[::1]:5173/` in your browser to play.

### Building for Production

```bash
# Create an optimized production build
npm run build

# Preview the production build locally
npm run preview
```

The production build outputs to the `dist/` directory, ready to be deployed to any static hosting service.

### Playing the Game

1. **Read the Intro** — Absorb the setting and understand what awaits your fighter in the Penal Arena.
2. **Review the Medical Dossier** — Study the injection protocols and degradation mechanics on the rules screen.
3. **Fight** — Choose your targets, manage your distance, and try to destroy your opponent before your own body destroys you. Each round gives you 5 actions.
4. **Survive the Pit Stop** — Between rounds, make an agonizing triage choice to keep your fighter functional.
5. **Endure or Triumph** — Knock out your opponent, or survive all 12 rounds of escalating biological horror.
6. **Face the Epilogue** — Your choices and your fighter's final state determine the branching narrative conclusion.
